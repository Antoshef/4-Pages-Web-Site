Teachers

The high quality of training at SoftUni is guaranteed by a carefully selected teaching staff. We believe that every child deserves educators to support and motivate him! Our team consists of professionals in the field of programming, specialists in working with children and motivated mentors.

Vyara Bogatewa

Director

Dr. Vyara Bogateva is the Educational Director of SoftUni Kids. She has a doctorate in the Department of Preschool Education at Sofia University "St. Kliment Ohridski ”and has completed a special research course in the doctoral program of the Institute of Education - University of London. Lecturer for over 3 years at Sofia University in the field of theory and technology of family education. She has a master's degree in preschool and primary school pedagogy and a bachelor's degree in special pedagogy. Vyara Bogateva is an experienced teacher with 10 years of work in the field of pedagogy, half of which as a manager of international institutions.

Wladimir Nikolow

Head-Teacher

Vladimir Nikolov is a team leader and lecturer at SoftUni Kids. He is a cultural anthropologist by education, and a primary school teacher and pedagogue by profession. His experience working with children and adolescents began in 2015. He has worked in various non-governmental organizations and public schools, and his goal has always been innovative educational change and providing quality and affordable education to both children and people working with them. It is this motivation and experience that makes him part of the SoftUni Kids initiative, where he sees an opportunity to contribute to the preparation of the new generation of Bulgaria for the challenges of the 21st century.
