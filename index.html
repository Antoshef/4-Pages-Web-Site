About SoftUni Kids

SoftUni Kids is an initiative of SoftUni aimed at the little ones. In order for each child to experience the process of creation through programming, our team of professionals has developed an innovative approach to learning through creation and continuous teamwork. At the heart of the curriculum are games as a natural way to acquire knowledge and skills.

SoftUni Kids offers programming training for children and students, graphic design, robotics, free programming lessons, demo lessons and more.

Learning programs

All children and students in SoftUni Kids have the opportunity to choose whether to study in open courses on various topics such as programming, design, robotics and / or to participate in one of the two programs - software and hardware. All courses and programs are suitable for children from preschool age to 6th grade, and parents can choose the appropriate group for their child so that he can learn among his peers.

Programming for children - is it a suitable choice for your child?

Many young children have answers to the question "what do I want to be when I grow up". It is enough, as parents, to observe the little man, his actions and interests, what attracts and holds his attention. If your child is not only having fun with technology and machines, but is also interested in how they work, it is a good idea to consider appropriate programming training for children. It is advisable to focus on a program that combines creative and analytical thinking, practical and personal skills. This is exactly what you will find in the overall curriculum offered by SoftUni Kids. It is based on the method of "learning by doing", as well as working in teams of several children. This helps the little ones to immediately put into practice what they have learned and at the same time develops them as team players. Detailed information on the complete programming program for children can be found here.

What programming training for children will you find with us?

If you are looking for a suitable option for programming training for children in Sofia, then we offer you two main options, from which to choose the right one for you.

  • The complete programming program for children from preschool to 6th grade. It consists of 5 levels, including hardware and software course, and always starts with the input module "First steps in programming".

  • Summer academies. They include different areas of study and will skillfully combine children's love of games with the acquisition of useful new knowledge.

And given that modern life requires flexible solutions in the direction of distance learning, you can also choose the option of programming for children online from anywhere in the world.

Free programming lessons for children

In case you prefer to check the qualities of the trainings with us before investing in them, our academy offers free programming lessons for children. During these demonstrations, led by top experts in the field, curious kids and you, as parents, gain personal impressions about the way of teaching and about the teachers themselves. This helps you make the most informed decision whether to enroll your child in the entire program or in one of our summer academies.

Why choose SoftUni Kids for comprehensive training and programming lessons for children?

As a responsible parent, you want your child to receive a quality education that provides them with long-term useful skills that they can put into practice. In SoftUni Kids you will find all this and more:

  • A well-structured curriculum using the "learning by doing" method, which starts with the introductory module "First steps in programming".

  • Free programming lessons for children in Sofia and online, based on which you assess whether you want your child to join a more in-depth training program.

  • Summer academies, combining the joy of the game with the satisfaction of learning new things.

  • Developing presentation and team skills in your child.

  • Competent teachers with an approach to children.
