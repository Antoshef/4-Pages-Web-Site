This is the second part of the online training "First steps in the program", which is supported and a basic online module during the programming training of SoftUni Kids. It aims to build basic tasks and skills to be used for children to keep their shoe in the next modules of the smallest programs. The first level in the direction of the software courses is required for the completed, subsequent completion of parts of the course and received certificates.

During the course "First steps in programming - part 2", some of them gain knowledge about your cycle, combination, and also participate in active group communication and teamwork - principles that can be included in the training program SoftUni Kids programming software.

The course ended with the creation of scenarios that can be modified, assembled and programmed by children. On the last day of hiring parents, parents are welcome in the last half hour to see and present their creation by their children program! After completing the second part of the course, children receive and have certificates for successfully passing the level "First steps in the programs".

Who is it suitable for?

The online course is suitable for complete beginners: children need to be able to read and write and have access to a computer with an Internet connection.

Learning program

Throughout the course we will have constant two-way communication with children. They will have the opportunity to ask us questions and communicate with each other (especially during breaks and after classes). We will have fun while learning how to program.
